About Us

Clash of Clans is a 2012 free-to-play mobile strategy video game developed and published by Finnish game developer Supercell. The game was released for iOS platforms on August 2, 2012, and on Google Play for Android on October 7, 2013.

Looking to join in EYG

1️⃣ Player looking to join an EYG Clan? Decided it looks more fun to be a member than a friend? Have another account that needs a home? React now to talk to a recruiter about the many opportunities we have available here at EYG. It will speed up the process if you have a screenshot of your clash profile & home base ready to show.

2️⃣ Player looking to join a Competitive League Team Are you a competitive juggernaut? Want to be one? If the need to compete on any level from novice to pro is in your blood, EYG has the clans for you. Our league players compete in ESL, CWL, GCC, Pinnacle Cup, MLCW, SAWL, AL and tons of other competitions and tournaments. We do friendly wars and friendly challenges daily to hone your skills and keep you on the cutting edge of the current meta's and base building defensive practices.

3️⃣ Clan Leader looking to merge into EYG Family Are you a leader of a clan looking to join the EYG family? React now to talk to our merger team to discuss the process and talk about advantages of becoming an EYG family clan. Minimum requirements must be met to start the process: Level 10+ ADULT clan, 40+ players, 100% fair play.
